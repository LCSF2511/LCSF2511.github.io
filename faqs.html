Frequently Asked Questions

1. What products do you offer?

We specialize in desk lamps, floor lamps, smart lighting systems, and accessories designed to enhance your space.

2. Do you offer warranty on your products?

Yes! All products come with a 1-year standard warranty. Extended warranty options are available for select items.

3. How can I place a custom order?

You can submit a special order request through our Contact Us page or call our customer service team.

4. Do you offer installation services?

Installation services are available for certain products, such as smart lighting systems. Contact us to schedule an appointment.

5. What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for most items. Products must be in original condition and packaging. Some exclusions apply.
